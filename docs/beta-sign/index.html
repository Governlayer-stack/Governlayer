1. Purpose

GovernLayer Inc. ("GovernLayer," "we," or "us") invites you ("Beta Tester," "you") to participate in a limited beta testing program for the GovernLayer AI Governance Platform (the "Platform"). This Agreement governs your participation in the beta program.

2. Beta Period

The beta testing period is approximately one (1) week from the date of signing, unless extended by mutual written agreement. Either party may terminate participation at any time with written notice (email is sufficient). Upon termination, you must cease use of the Platform and any beta materials.

3. What You Get

  • Full access to all GovernLayer Platform features during the beta period
  • Direct access to the GovernLayer engineering team for support and feedback
  • No charge for beta access -- the Platform is provided free of charge during the beta period
  • Early insight into the product roadmap and upcoming features

4. What We Ask

  • Use non-production or synthetic data only -- do not run the Platform against live production systems containing real customer data
  • Provide honest, constructive feedback on your experience with the Platform
  • Report any bugs, errors, or unexpected behavior you encounter
  • Complete a brief exit survey at the conclusion of the beta period
  • Participate in at least one feedback call (15-30 minutes) during the beta period

  • You retain full ownership of all data you submit to the Platform

  • Access to beta tester data is restricted to GovernLayer engineering personnel only, solely for the purpose of providing support and improving the Platform
