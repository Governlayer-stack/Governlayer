Frequently asked questions

What frameworks do you support?
29 frameworks including SOC 2, GDPR, ISO 27001, EU AI Act, HIPAA, NIST AI RMF, NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 42001, PCI DSS v4, CCPA, NIS2, DORA, DSA, DMA, and more. Each framework comes with full control mapping, gap analysis, and evidence tracking.
How does drift detection work?
We analyze AI reasoning traces using sentence-transformer embeddings compared against safety manifolds. When an agent's behavior drifts beyond safe boundaries, we calculate a drift coefficient and can automatically veto dangerous outputs before they reach users.
Is the audit ledger really tamper-proof?
Yes. Every governance decision is SHA-256 hash-chained, meaning each record includes the hash of the previous record. Any modification to historical records breaks the chain, making tampering immediately detectable. This is the same principle used in blockchain technology.
Can I use GovernLayer with my existing AI stack?
Absolutely. GovernLayer is API-first — it works with any AI framework, model provider, or deployment platform. Send your reasoning traces to our API, and we handle governance, compliance, and auditing. We also offer an MCP server for IDE integration.
What happens when a high-risk decision is detected?
High-risk decisions are automatically escalated to human reviewers via our Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) workflow. Reviewers receive email notifications with full context, SLA deadlines, and can approve, modify, or reject the decision. Everything is logged in the audit ledger.
How long does it take to get audit-ready?
Most teams reach 80%+ readiness within 4-8 weeks using GovernLayer, compared to 6-12 months manually. Our auto-generated policies, control mapping, and evidence tracking eliminate the manual busywork that typically slows compliance programs.
