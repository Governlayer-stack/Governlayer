## 1. PURPOSE

GovernLayer is providing early access to its AI governance platform (the "Platform") for evaluation and testing purposes. This Beta Testing Agreement ("Agreement") governs the terms under which the Beta Participant ("Participant") may access and use the Platform during the beta period.

## 2. BETA PERIOD

The beta period begins on the Effective Date and continues until:
- (a) GovernLayer notifies the Participant in writing of termination, or
- (b) GovernLayer launches the Platform for general availability ("GA"), or
- (c) Either party terminates this Agreement with 7 days' written notice.

## 3. ACCESS AND USE

### 3.1 License Grant
GovernLayer grants the Participant a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to access and use the Platform solely for internal evaluation and testing during the beta period.

### 3.2 Restrictions
The Participant shall NOT:
- (a) Use beta outputs as the sole basis for real compliance certifications, regulatory filings, or audit submissions;
- (b) Resell, sublicense, or provide access to any third party;
- (c) Reverse engineer, decompile, or attempt to extract source code from the Platform;
- (d) Perform load testing, stress testing, or security testing without prior written approval;
- (e) Use the Platform to process data subject to regulatory requirements (e.g., real PHI under HIPAA, real PII under GDPR) unless explicitly agreed in writing.

### 3.3 Beta Disclaimer
The Platform is provided in **beta form**. Features may be incomplete, contain bugs, or change without notice. The Participant acknowledges that the Platform is not yet production-ready and should not be relied upon for production governance decisions.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 4.1 Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:
- Platform features, architecture, and technical capabilities
- Pricing, roadmap, and business strategy
- API specifications, documentation, and endpoints
- Any non-public information shared during the beta period

### 4.2 Obligations
Each party agrees to:
- (a) Keep Confidential Information strictly confidential;
- (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent;
- (c) Use Confidential Information only for purposes of this Agreement;
- (d) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.

### 4.3 Exceptions
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- (a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party;
- (b) Was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure;
- (c) Is independently developed without use of the disclosing party's Confidential Information;
- (d) Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order (with prompt notice to the disclosing party).

### 4.4 Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **two (2) years**.

## 5. FEEDBACK AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 5.1 Feedback License
The Participant may provide suggestions, bug reports, feature requests, and other feedback ("Feedback") during the beta period. The Participant hereby grants GovernLayer a **perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free, worldwide license** to use, modify, and incorporate any Feedback into the Platform or any other GovernLayer product without obligation or compensation.

### 5.2 Ownership
GovernLayer retains all rights, title, and interest in the Platform, including all intellectual property rights. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any ownership rights to the Participant.

### 5.3 Participant Data
The Participant retains all rights to data they input into the Platform ("Participant Data"). GovernLayer will not use Participant Data for any purpose other than providing and improving the Platform during the beta period.

## 6. DATA HANDLING

### 6.1 Test Data
GovernLayer strongly recommends the Participant use **synthetic or anonymized test data** during the beta period. If the Participant chooses to use real data, they do so at their own risk.

### 6.2 Data Retention
Upon termination of the beta period or upon the Participant's written request, GovernLayer will delete all Participant Data within **thirty (30) days**.

### 6.3 Security
GovernLayer will implement reasonable security measures to protect Participant Data, including encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest. However, as a beta product, GovernLayer does not guarantee the same security standards as its general availability release.

## 9. PUBLICITY

### 9.1 No Public Statements
Neither party shall issue press releases or public statements referencing the other party's participation in the beta program without prior written consent.

### 9.2 Case Study
Upon mutual agreement, and only after the beta period, GovernLayer may reference the Participant as a beta design partner in marketing materials. Any such reference requires the Participant's prior written approval of the specific language used.

## 10. TERMINATION

### 10.1 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with **seven (7) days' written notice** to the other party.

### 10.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately if the other party materially breaches any term and fails to cure such breach within **fourteen (14) days** of written notice.

### 10.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- (a) The Participant's access to the Platform will be revoked;
- (b) The Participant shall cease all use of the Platform and Confidential Information;
- (c) GovernLayer will delete Participant Data per Section 6.2;
- (d) Sections 4, 5, 7, 8, and 11 shall survive termination.
