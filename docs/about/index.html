Our Approach

Three Pillars of Autonomous Governance

We didn't bolt AI features onto a GRC tool. We built governance from first principles for the agentic AI era.

In-Decision-Path

Governance happens during AI decisions, not after. Real-time monitoring and enforcement in the inference pipeline. Every decision is assessed before it reaches the end user.

Cryptographic Proof

Every governance decision is SHA-256 hash-chained into a tamper-proof audit ledger. Auditors can mathematically verify your entire governance history with a single API call.

Multi-Model Consensus

No single point of failure. 14 models across local and cloud providers, 3 consensus strategies (Voting, Chain-of-Verification, Adversarial Debate) eliminate hallucination risk.
