Use Cases

How enterprises use GovernLayer

Real scenarios across regulated industries.

Financial Services

Governing Loan Approval AI

A bank routes every loan decision through GovernLayer. Drift detection catches when the model starts approving riskier profiles. The 6-dimension risk score flags autonomy and fairness concerns. Every decision is hash-chained to the audit ledger.

Outcome: OCC auditor verifies the entire governance history with a single API call. Zero manual evidence compilation.
Healthcare

Clinical Decision Support Compliance

A hospital governs its clinical AI systems. Air-gapped deployment with Ollama means zero PHI leaves the network. HIPAA compliance documentation is auto-generated. Behavioral drift alerts fire when the model's recommendations shift.

Outcome: HIPAA audit completed in days instead of months. Continuous monitoring replaces annual point-in-time reviews.
HR and Recruiting

Shadow AI Discovery in Hiring

GovernLayer's agent discovery scan finds unregistered GPT-4 usage in the marketing department running resume screening. The fairness analysis flags gender disparity. Policy engine blocks biased outputs automatically.

Outcome: NYC LL144 compliance documentation generated automatically. Shadow AI registered, governed, and compliant within one week.
Enterprise AI Platform

Governing 50+ AI Agents at Scale

A CTO registers all AI agents — customer chatbots, code reviewers, data pipelines, fraud detectors. The executive dashboard shows governance health across the entire fleet. Role-based views give each team what they need.

Outcome: Board-ready governance report generated on demand. Mean time to detect ungoverned AI: less than 24 hours.
EU-Regulated Company

EU AI Act Compliance Before Enforcement

A European company needs full EU AI Act compliance before August 2026. GovernLayer's compliance hub creates the program, auto-generates required policies, schedules quarterly audits, and continuously monitors all high-risk AI systems.

Outcome: Audit-ready EU AI Act compliance package exported in one click. Continuous monitoring ensures no compliance gaps between audits.
Multi-Vendor AI Operations

Cross-Provider Governance

A company using OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and internal Ollama models. Vendor risk management scores each provider. The multi-LLM consensus engine cross-validates critical outputs — no single hallucinating model corrupts compliance.

Outcome: Vendor risk dashboard shows real-time posture across all providers. Critical decisions validated by 3+ models before execution.
Government and Defense

Classified AI Governance

Air-gapped deployment with local Ollama inference. Zero external API calls. Full audit trail for classified AI systems. FedRAMP and NIST CSF compliance auto-documented.

Outcome: Complete AI governance with zero data exfiltration risk. Auditors verify compliance without accessing classified systems directly.
Insurance

Claims Processing AI Governance

An insurer governs AI-powered claims assessment. Risk scoring ensures fairness across demographics. The audit ledger proves to regulators that every claim decision was governed. Incident management tracks when the model flags unusual patterns.

Outcome: Regulatory examination completed with zero findings. Continuous control monitoring catches drift before it impacts policyholders.

Deploy how you need to

Cloud, self-hosted, or air-gapped. Your data, your infrastructure, your rules.

Cloud SaaS

Managed deployment on Railway. SOC 2 compliant infrastructure. TLS 1.2+, encrypted at rest. Zero ops required.

Self-Hosted

Docker Compose or Kubernetes. Full control over data residency. Includes PostgreSQL, Redis, and optional Ollama for local LLM.
