The Solution

Governance That Runs Itself

GovernLayer is the first fully autonomous AI governance platform — continuously monitoring, auditing, and enforcing compliance across 29 frameworks without human intervention.

17 Integrated Services — One API

1. AI Governance Engine

Real-time governance on every AI output. Drift detection, risk scoring, policy enforcement, and audit logging via POST /v1/govern, /risk, /drift, /scan.

2. Model & Agent Registry

Central inventory of every AI model and agent. Shadow AI auto-discovery, governance cards, risk tiers, approval workflows, and dependency graphs.

3. Compliance Hub (29 Frameworks)

Auto-generate policies, track readiness scores, schedule audits, and export audit-ready packages. EU AI Act, NIST, ISO 42001, HIPAA, SOC 2, and 24 more.

4. Evidence Auto-Collection

Connect to GitHub, Jira, cloud platforms. Scheduled evidence gathering with coverage mapping shows auditors exactly what is documented and what is missing.

5. Tamper-Proof Audit Ledger

SHA-256 hash-chained audit trail. Every governance decision cryptographically linked. Auditors verify the entire chain with one API call.

6. Multi-LLM Consensus (Achonye)

14 models, 3 hallucination-resistance strategies: Voting, Chain-of-Verification, Adversarial Debate. Zero single points of failure.

7. Continuous Control Monitoring

Map controls to regulatory requirements. Automated gap analysis, framework-specific views, and real-time alerts on control failures.

8. Policy Engine

Configurable rules with allow/warn/block enforcement actions. Default-deny posture with full enforcement audit trail on every decision.

9. 6-Dimension Risk Scoring

Deterministic scoring: privacy, autonomy, infrastructure, oversight, transparency, fairness. No LLM hallucinations — pure calculation.

10. Threat Intelligence

MITRE ATLAS threat analysis, OWASP AI security scans, incident response playbooks, and jurisdiction-specific regulatory deadline tracking.

11. Vendor Risk Management

Assess third-party AI vendors. Risk matrix, automated questionnaires, and continuous provider monitoring across your entire AI supply chain.

12. Incident Management

Create, track, and resolve AI-related incidents. Severity-based triage with Slack, Jira, and ServiceNow integrations.

13. Analytics & Reporting

Fairness analysis, explainability (XAI), data drift detection, security scanning, and custom compliance reports for any framework.

14. Knowledge Graph

Regulatory relationship mapping. Control-to-regulation links, gap analysis intelligence, and AI-powered regulatory advisory.

15. Enterprise Multi-Tenancy

Organizations, API keys, SSO, RBAC, and role-based dashboards for 5 personas: Engineering, Compliance, Executive, Security, Data Science.

16. Human-in-the-Loop Escalation

Auto-escalation when AI decisions exceed risk thresholds. Review queue with SLA tracking and resolution workflows.

17. AGI Safety Controls

Recursive self-improvement monitoring (RSIM), capability containment (CCV), deceptive alignment detection (DAD), and multi-agent coordination safety (MACM). Future-ready.
