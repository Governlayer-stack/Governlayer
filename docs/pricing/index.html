Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we prorate the difference.

What happens if I exceed my rate limit?

Requests over your limit return a 429 status. We never charge overage fees — upgrade when ready.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans get 2 months free. Contact us for enterprise annual agreements.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What compliance frameworks are supported?

SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, FedRAMP, PCI DSS, and CCPA. Custom frameworks available on Pro and Enterprise.
