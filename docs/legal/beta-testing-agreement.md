## 3. What You Get

## 6. Data Handling

- **Your Data:** You retain full ownership of all data you submit to the platform.
- **Storage:** Data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted on Railway (US infrastructure).
- **Access:** Only GovernLayer engineering staff may access your data, solely for debugging and platform improvement.

- **No Selling:** We will never sell, share, or monetize your data.
