What Makes Us Different

We didn't bolt AI features onto a GRC tool

GovernLayer was built from scratch for autonomous AI governance — not retrofitted from compliance checklists.

Real-Time vs. Point-in-Time

GovernLayer: In-the-decision-path governance. Drift detection, risk scoring, and policy veto happen before the AI output reaches users.
Traditional tools: Retroactive audits and snapshot compliance. Problems discovered weeks or months after damage is done.

Cryptographic Proof vs. PDF Reports

GovernLayer: SHA-256 hash-chained ledger. Every governance decision cryptographically linked. Auditors verify with one API call.
Traditional tools: Generate PDF reports and screenshots. Evidence is self-reported and manually compiled.

Multi-LLM Consensus vs. Single Model

GovernLayer: 14 models with 3 consensus strategies — Voting, Chain-of-Verification, Adversarial Debate. No single point of hallucination failure.
Competitors: Single model assessments with no cross-validation. One hallucinating model corrupts your compliance record.

Fail-Closed vs. Fail-Open

GovernLayer: When systems degrade, governance defaults to CAUTION — blocking risky decisions until full assessment is available.
Traditional tools: When monitoring fails, AI continues ungoverned. Gaps in coverage go undetected.
