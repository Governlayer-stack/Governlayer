WHY GOVERNLAYER

Real governance. Cryptographic proof.

The era of checkbox compliance is over. Enterprises need governance they can verify, not reports they have to trust.

Tamper-Proof Audit Trail

Every governance decision is SHA-256 hash-chained into an immutable ledger. If anyone alters a record, the chain breaks. No rubber stamps. No fabricated evidence.

Real-Time, Not Retroactive

GovernLayer monitors AI agents as they run — detecting drift, scoring risk, and enforcing policies in real time. Not generating reports after the fact.

Multi-LLM Consensus

Critical decisions require 3+ independent models to agree. Voting, Chain-of-Verification, and Adversarial Debate eliminate single-model hallucination risk.

PLATFORM

Complete AI governance in one platform

Everything you need to govern AI at enterprise scale — from agent discovery to compliance reporting.

Agent Registry & Shadow AI

Discover, register, and govern every AI agent across your organization. Detect shadow AI usage before it becomes a risk.

GET /v1/agents?status=unregistered&risk=high

Behavioral Drift Detection

Sentence-transformer embeddings detect when AI behavior drifts from approved boundaries. Automatic alerts and blocking.

POST /v1/drift/detect {"behavior": "...", "baseline": "..."}

Multi-Framework Compliance

18 regulatory report generators covering EU AI Act, NIST, ISO 42001, GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, and 12 more frameworks.

GET /v1/reports?framework=eu-ai-act&format=pdf

Risk Scoring Engine

6-dimension deterministic risk scoring: Privacy, Autonomy, Infrastructure, Oversight, Transparency, Fairness.

POST /v1/risk-score {"handles_pii": true, "autonomous": false}

Immutable Audit Ledger

SHA-256 hash-chained audit trail. Every governance decision cryptographically linked. Tamper-evident by design.

GET /v1/ledger?verify=true {"chain_valid": true}

Policy Engine

Define governance rules, evaluate AI systems against policies, and enforce compliance automatically across all agents.

POST /v1/policies/evaluate {"agent_id": "...", "action": "..."}
