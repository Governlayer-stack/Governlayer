FAQ

Common questions

AI governance is the process of monitoring, auditing, and controlling how AI systems make decisions. As regulations like the EU AI Act come into force, organizations deploying AI agents need documentation of what their models are doing, proof that decisions are unbiased, and the ability to detect when behavior drifts from approved boundaries. Without governance, you're exposed to regulatory fines, legal liability, and reputational risk.

GovernLayer uses sentence-transformer embeddings to create a "safety manifold" for each use case. Every AI decision is compared against this manifold in real time. When behavior drifts beyond approved boundaries, GovernLayer can alert your team, flag the decision for review, or automatically veto it — before harm reaches your users.

No — GovernLayer complements your compliance team, not replaces them. We automate the continuous monitoring, evidence collection, and report generation that would otherwise take weeks of manual work. Your compliance team or consultant still makes strategic decisions, but GovernLayer gives them real-time visibility and auditor-ready documentation.

Every governance decision is SHA-256 hash-chained — each record contains the hash of the previous record, creating an immutable chain. If anyone alters a single record, all subsequent hashes break. This gives you cryptographic proof of your entire AI governance history, similar to how blockchain ensures data integrity.
