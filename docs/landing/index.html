Frequently asked questions

AI governance is the process of monitoring, auditing, and controlling how AI systems make decisions. Regulations like the EU AI Act require documentation, bias proof, and drift detection — with fines up to €35M for non-compliance.

Vanta automates IT compliance (SOC 2, ISO) but doesn't understand AI behavior. Credo AI focuses on pre-deployment model assessments. GovernLayer sits in the decision path — governing AI agents in real time with cryptographic proof, multi-LLM consensus, and behavioral drift detection. We're the only platform where an auditor can mathematically verify your entire governance history.

We use sentence-transformer embeddings to create safety manifolds for each use case. Every AI decision is compared against this manifold in real time. When behavior drifts beyond approved boundaries, GovernLayer can alert, flag for review, or automatically veto.

Every governance decision is SHA-256 hash-chained — each record contains the hash of the previous record. Altering any record breaks the chain. One API call verifies the integrity of your entire audit history.
